Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 May 2026

1. Who We Are

PhysicianWealth ("we", "us", "our") operates physician-wealth.com. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

2. Data We Collect

Account data: When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name.

Usage data: We collect anonymised analytics (pages visited, features used, session duration) to improve the service.

Financial data you enter: Salary, debt, investment, and tax figures you input into our calculators and tools are stored in your account to power personalised projections. We never share this data with third parties.

Payment data: Payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your full card number. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment processing.

Cookies: We use essential cookies for authentication and optional analytics cookies. See our Cookie Policy.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to: provide and maintain the service, process payments, send account-related emails (password resets, billing), improve the product based on anonymised usage patterns, and generate personalised financial projections using AI models.

We do NOT sell your data. We do NOT share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. AI Processing

Some features use AI models to analyse your financial inputs and generate recommendations. Your data is sent to our AI processing pipeline (which may include third-party AI providers) solely to generate your results. We do not use your data to train AI models. AI-generated content is for informational purposes only and does not constitute financial advice.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Supabase (hosted on AWS). We use row-level security (RLS) to ensure users can only access their own data. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to: access your data, correct inaccurate data, delete your data ("right to be forgotten"), export your data (data portability), object to processing, withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, email hello@physician-wealth.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days. Anonymised analytics data may be retained indefinitely.

8. Third-Party Services

We use: Stripe (payments), Supabase (database and auth), Cloudflare Pages (hosting). Each has its own privacy policy governing their processing of data.

9. Children

Our service is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated via email or in-app notice.

11. Contact

For privacy questions: hello@physician-wealth.com
