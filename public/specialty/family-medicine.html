Compensation by Experience Level

Family medicine starting salaries are $230,000-$260,000 and grow modestly to $280,000-$320,000 at mid-career. Rural positions offer $300,000-$380,000+ with loan repayment. DPC (direct primary care) practices are emerging as a high-satisfaction, competitive-income alternative to traditional fee-for-service.

Top-Paying States

Rural and underserved areas offer the best FM compensation, often with NHSC loan repayment ($50,000 for 2 years). Texas, the Midwest, and Southeast have the most FM openings with highest COL-adjusted pay. Urban saturated markets (NYC, LA, SF) pay the least relative to cost of living.

Employed vs Private Practice

Employed FM physicians earn $240,000-$280,000. Private practice FM with a panel of 2,000-2,500 patients earns $280,000-$350,000. DPC practices with 600-800 members at $100-$150/month can generate $300,000-$400,000 with dramatically better work-life balance and no insurance billing.

How to Maximize Your Compensation

Three highest-leverage actions: (1) Consider DPC - many FM physicians earn more with fewer patients and zero insurance hassle. (2) Stack loan repayment programs (NHSC, state, employer) - $100,000-$250,000 in debt elimination is equivalent to $150,000-$400,000 pre-tax income.
