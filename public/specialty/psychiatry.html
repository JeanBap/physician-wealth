Compensation by Experience Level

Starting psychiatrist salaries are $260,000-$300,000 and grow steadily to $350,000-$400,000+ with a full panel. Telepsychiatry has opened geographic flexibility and multi-state practice. Child and adolescent psychiatrists earn 10-15% premiums due to even greater scarcity. Addiction psychiatry and consultation-liaison are growing subspecialties.

Top-Paying States

Psychiatry shortage exists in virtually every state. Rural and underserved areas pay 30-50% premiums. States with strong telepsychiatry laws (many Southeastern and Western states) allow multi-state practice from a single location, effectively multiplying earning potential.

Employed vs Private Practice

Employed psychiatrists earn $280,000-$340,000. Private practice psychiatrists focusing on cash-pay psychotherapy and medication management earn $350,000-$500,000+. Telepsychiatry practices with multi-state licensure can achieve very high hourly rates of $250-$350/hr with minimal overhead.

How to Maximize Your Compensation

Three highest-leverage actions: (1) Build a cash-pay practice component - many patients prefer private-pay psychiatry. (2) Obtain multi-state licensure for telepsychiatry.
